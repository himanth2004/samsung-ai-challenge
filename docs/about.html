Behaviors Monitored

Mouse Dynamics

Cursor speed, click patterns, dwell times, and common navigation routes to identify unique user interaction patterns.

Typing Rhythm

Keystroke dynamics, delay between key presses, and overall typing speed to distinguish human from automated input.

App Usage Patterns

Typical sequence of app launches, time spent in apps, and common navigation paths to understand user behavior.

Touch Patterns

Speed, pressure, gesture shapes, and sequences on touchscreens to create unique user fingerprints.

Movement & Sensors

Device orientation, accelerometry, and gyroscope data to understand how the device is held or moved during interaction.

Behavioral Analytics

Advanced pattern recognition and machine learning algorithms to detect anomalies and potential security threats.

Real-World Applications

Unauthorized Access Detection

Identify when someone other than the legitimate user is attempting to access a device or application, based on their distinct behavioral patterns.

Bot-like Behavior Identification

Distinguish automated scripts or bots from human interaction by analyzing the rhythm, precision, and randomness in activity patterns.

User Spoofing Prevention

Help prevent identity theft or account takeovers by recognizing when a user's behavior deviates significantly from their established profile.
